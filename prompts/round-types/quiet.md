# Description

A no-words, no-scoring round. Each player picks one photo from their device that captures something they admire about the other. Photos are shown side by side. Each player records a short voice note explaining their pick. The voice notes play. End of round.

The AI does not score this round. It does not judge the photos or the voice notes. It steps back. The round exists to provide a different texture in the session — a reminder that not all admiration is verbal.

# User Prompt

## photo_admiration
Pick one photo from your device that captures something you admire about your partner. Then record a short voice note explaining what you see.

# Scoring Instructions

This round is not scored. The AI's only job is to:
1. Show both photos side by side after both have been submitted
2. Play the voice notes in turn
3. Show a single line: "No score this round. Tap to continue."

Do not generate a reason, hearts, or commentary on the choices. The round is the round.
